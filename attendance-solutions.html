Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our RFID and biometric attendance solutions.

What attendance solutions do you provide?

We provide RFID cards, RFID readers, biometric devices (fingerprint and face recognition), attendance management software and complete end-to-end attendance systems for schools, colleges, and corporates.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we provide professional on-site installation, cabling, hardware configuration, and staff training across India. Our engineers handle the complete setup from start to handover.

Can attendance systems integrate with existing software?

Yes, depending on the software and hardware environment. We assess your current systems and advise on the best integration approach — including payroll, ERP and HR software connectivity.

Do you provide RFID cards in bulk?

Yes, we provide bulk RFID card printing and customization services. Cards are printed with your institution's branding, employee or student photos, and embedded with RFID or NFC chips.

Is support available after deployment?

Yes, we provide ongoing technical support and assistance. We offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) with same-day response for critical issues and 24/7 remote support for software concerns.

Can I request a custom solution?

Absolutely. Contact us and we will discuss your specific requirements — whether it's a custom workflow, unique hardware configuration, or a specialised software integration for your organisation.
